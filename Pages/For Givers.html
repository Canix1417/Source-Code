Here at 28:19 we believe that supporters play an incredible role in spreading the gospel.

When you give to someone in need, you submit yourself to Jesus' character over your own. Additionally, you are sharing in the missionary work of the individual you supported. Let's say the missionary you donate to goes onto to show Christ to 5 individuals who accept Jesus as their savior, we believe that you played a role in bringing those individuals to Christ and therefor, have built up reward in Heaven for your obedience.

Giving Testimonies!

Giving was something I always knew I should be doing but never actually put into practice until recently. Before, I was operating out of a fear of lacking. Let's face it, giving graciously can often seem counter-culture in this day in age. Sure, there are amazing websites and programs out there where individuals can go and give to others. The reason why giving felt counter-cultural to me and could feel that way to you is because of how media portrays self-love and happiness that can only be found in consumption. Think about it, how often do commercials, songs, and other forms of media hint (or outright say) that you would be happier and more fulfilled if you just had more.
