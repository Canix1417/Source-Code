About 28:19

28:19 is an online space that connects those in Christian ministry in need of financial aid to people of varying backgrounds who are looking to give.
If you wish to start giving encouragement and financial support to a fundraiser(s), you'll need to follow these easy process of creating an account.
To start your own fundraiser on 28:19, create an account with us and then go to the For Missionaries tab. Don't worry, we try to make the account and fundraiser set up process super simple!

To find out more about our team and how we got started, we invite you to look at our About Us Pages.
